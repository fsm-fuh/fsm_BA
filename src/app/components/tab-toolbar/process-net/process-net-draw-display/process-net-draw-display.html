Drawing Area

Drag and drop elements from the display area above to create your process net.

Left-click and drag elements to move them around.

Middle-click a place or transition to delete it (connections removed automatically).

Right-click one place or transition to select, then right-click the opposite type to connect them.

Left-click a connection to decrease its weight (minimum 1, hidden at 1). Right-click to increase its weight.

Middle-click a connection to delete it.
